Our Story

Redefining Figure Skating in the Mile High City

Founded in 2019, the Crystal Collective emerged from a vision to create a world-class figure skating ensemble in Denver. What began as a small group of passionate skaters has evolved into one of the most respected programs in North American figure skating.

We share Ball Arena with the Denver Hellhoundsâ€”a partnership that, while occasionally challenging, represents the diverse athletic spirit of our city. Our skaters train alongside hockey players, each pushing the boundaries of what's possible on ice.
